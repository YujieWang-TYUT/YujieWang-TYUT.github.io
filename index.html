About Me

I am a graduate student in power electronics and energy systems at Taiyuan University of Technology. My research focuses on grid-forming converters, virtual synchronous generators (VSG), photovoltaic power systems, and microgrid stability and control.

Research Interests

  • Grid-forming Inverters (Virtual Synchronous Generator, VSG)
  • Parallel Inverter Circulating Current Suppression
  • Photovoltaic Power Forecasting
  • Energy Storage and Microgrid Control
  • Power Electronics Modeling and Simulation

Projects

  • VSG-based Parallel Inverter Control Strategy
  • Adaptive Virtual Harmonic Impedance Control
  • Photovoltaic Probabilistic Forecasting (ZM-QR)
  • FPGA-based Digital Power Converter Control

Publications

  • Y. Wang, et al. Adaptive Virtual Harmonic Impedance Control for Parallel Inverters. (in preparation)
  • Y. Wang, et al. Zero-Mask Quantile Regression for Photovoltaic Forecasting. (in preparation)
