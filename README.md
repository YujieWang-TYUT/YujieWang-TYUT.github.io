## About Me

I am a graduate student in power electronics and energy systems at Taiyuan University of Technology.  
My research focuses on grid-forming converters, virtual synchronous generators (VSG), photovoltaic power systems, and microgrid stability and control.

---

## Research Interests

- Grid-forming Inverters (Virtual Synchronous Generator, VSG)  
- Parallel Inverter Circulating Current Suppression  
- Photovoltaic Power Forecasting  
- Energy Storage and Microgrid Control  
- Power Electronics Modeling and Simulation
